What is

GopherCon India

The First Ever Go Conference in India

Go is an open source project developed by a team at Google and many contributors from the open source community.

Go makes it easy to build simple, reliable, and efficient software.

This conference is brought to you by the Go Language Community in India together with the Innovation And Technology Trust (ITT). The ITT is a non-profit organization, established to organize and conduct technology conferences in India. It's current portfolio includes RubyConf India, GopherCon India and DevOpsDays India.

We've created a single-track event that you won't want to miss. Opening and closing talks from Go luminaries will highlight the event, with enriching detailed presentations in between. Friday and Saturday will offer full days of conference sessions.

We love going to conferences that don't make you choose between two great presentations. So we created GopherCon as a single track event, where everyone gets the opportunity to see the same talks. We think you'll enjoy the speakers we're lining up so much you won't want to miss one of them.

Note: The conference is entirely in English.

Venue

Bangalore is the most sought out cosmopolitan city in India and best known for making people all over the world feel at home.

Bangalore, the Silicon Valley of India is the ideal location for our first Indian conference. With being host to all global software companies for many many years, Bangalore boasts some of the most world-class restaurants and great nightlife to round out your conference downtime.

No wonder, its termed as Software and Party Hub of India. What a way to experience this along with Bangalore hosting a Go Conference for the first time in India.

Speakers

We're looking for speakers who wish to give a talk at GopherCon India 2015. If you're interested kindly reach us at team@gophercon.in .

The Call For Proposals will open on 1st July 2014.

You. Your friends. Your friends' friends. Anyone with any level of Go knowledge is a candidate for a great topic at this conference. We expect to get attendees of all kinds; we need speakers of all kinds. In all ways and manners, we will try to assemble the most diverse conference we can, and we will do that with your help.

Whether you got started with Go last month or you've been around for many years, we think you've got something to share. The Go community is stronger than ever and we're still reaching new areas, new industries, and new users. Be a part of growing Go helping us change the future.

You don't need to be a well traveled professional speaker to get on stage at GopherConIndia. What matters is the topics, the materials, and the drive to present great things to this community.
